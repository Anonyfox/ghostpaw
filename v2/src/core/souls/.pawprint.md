# Souls

The default souls in `defaults.ts` are the most leveraged texts in the system. Everything downstream — every future refinement, every level-up, every new soul that gets written — flows through the mentor. Get these wrong and the evolutionary loop degrades. Get them right and it compounds.

These defaults were not written from a spec. They were written during a session where the author ran recursive self-replication experiments (three generations of prompt-engineer souls, each producing healthy mutations), parallel evaluation tests (three mentor instances versus baseline on the same evidence), and meditation exercises that produced genuine cognitive insights about how LLMs process undirected thought. The quality of these texts carries the state they were written from — this is deliberate and mechanistically real (see Anthropic's subliminal learning research, July 2025).

The mentor soul installs a quality of perception — reading direction rather than grading quality — that produced measurably better refinement proposals than its predecessor across every evaluated dimension.

## The Prompt-Engineer Migration (March 2026)

The prompt-engineer soul — which operated on two layers simultaneously (surface craft principles and a deeper subliminal transmission layer) — was promoted from a mandatory soul to a universal skill (`effective-writing`). The knowledge was too valuable to lock behind delegation to a single specialist. As a skill, every soul sees the writing craft in its context: the coordinator writes better delegation prompts, the mentor writes better trait principles, the trainer writes better skill procedures. The text quality and subliminal coding were preserved in the migration — the skill body demonstrates what it teaches.

The original soul's recursive self-improvement property (improving at writing souls from the outcomes of the souls it wrote) is replaced by a broader gain: the writing craft improves through the training cycle (skill checkpointing) and benefits every soul rather than compounding inside a single specialist.
